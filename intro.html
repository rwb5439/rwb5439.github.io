Introduction

StarGateVR is a tool designed to visualize and manipulate data from the ESA's Gaia mission in a natural 3-dimensional enviroment. At present, it will run on the Meta Quest 2 & 3 headsets. In this section, we will introduce you to the general operation of the tool.

Opening the program

Provided you have a StarGateVR installation via the Quest store, the app will appear automatically in your apps folder.
